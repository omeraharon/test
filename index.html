The Project

I-TAL-YA Books aims to create one centralized catalogue for
Hebrew book holdings from across Italy, for scholars and
researchers around the world.

Our Goal
35,000

The entire catalogue will include an estimated 35,00 volumes from 14 Jewish communities and 25 state institutions across Italy

About us

Creating the Union Catalogue of Hebrew Books in Italy as a partnership between the Union of Italian Jewish Communities, the National Central Library of Rome and the National Library of Israel.
